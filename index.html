FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is included in the Registered Office plan?

It includes your Chelmsford registered office address plus handling of official HMRC and Companies House mail, with official scans included subject to fair use.

Does Registered Office include general business mail?

No. General business mail acceptance starts with our Virtual Office plan.

Can I collect parcels directly instead of paying for forwarding?

Yes, eligible items on Virtual Office and Business Infrastructure can be set up for direct collection, subject to branch and collection rules.

Is postage included?

No. Postage, courier charges and packaging materials are charged separately where forwarding is requested.
